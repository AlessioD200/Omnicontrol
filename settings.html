Integration Targets

  • Bluetooth displays (Samsung, LG, Sony)
  • Infrared receivers via USB IR blaster
  • Apple HomeKit lights, plugs, and scenes
  • Zigbee or Z-Wave domotica bridges (future)

Security Checklist

  • Rotate HomeKit bridge codes every 90 days.
  • Enable remote access only behind VPN or WireGuard.
  • Back up Omnicontrol configuration via `scp` to NAS.
  • Stay on the stable firmware channel unless testing new features.
